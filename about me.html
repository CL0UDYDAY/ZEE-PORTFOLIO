Ann Chealzee Nerida

My name is Ann Chealzee M. Nerida, and I would describe my branding as having a lively and playful vibe that permeates everything I do. I want to excite and cheer up everyone I come into contact with, especially when working with various clients, starting with my logo's friendly appearance.

In spite of my playful branding, I manage and maintain my branding by being serious about learning and listening. I also firmly think that brilliant ideas may originate from everywhere, so I'm constantly open to soaking up fresh concepts, advice, and ideas from my partners and clients. I always appreciate the chance to bring many viewpoints into the creative process, whether it be through meetings, direct criticism, or cooperative initiatives.

As we think of a cloud as being like a sponge, I am prepared to take in creativity and inspiration from wherever, and show it through my works.



Her forte in the art industry is graphic desiging, photo and video editing, digital arts, and watercolor painting
